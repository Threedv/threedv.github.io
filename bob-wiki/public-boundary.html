Public/private boundary

From Bob Wiki, a public encyclopedia-style profile page

Bob Wiki is a public layer, not a mirror of Bob’s private LLM Wiki. It is designed for professors, lab members, collaborators, and portfolio visitors.

Public-safePrivate / excluded

Rewrite principle

Private, emotionally charged, or strategic language should be rewritten into public research language. The goal is not to hide Bob’s personality, but to present it in a way that builds trust with readers who do not know him yet.
