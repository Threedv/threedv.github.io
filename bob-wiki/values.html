Values and operating principles

From Bob Wiki, a public encyclopedia-style profile page

Bob’s values are not presented as abstract self-help slogans. They are operating principles meant to produce finished artifacts, long-term skill, and generous impact.

Core values

  • Become a high-level AI researcher and builder.
  • Stay generous and useful to others.
  • Train both body and mind.
  • Pursue freedom through skill, code, research, and leverage.
  • Turn excitement into artifacts rather than only imagination.

Operating principles

PrincipleMeaningBehavioral translation
Radical ConsistencyCompounding comes from repeated contact with the work.Touch important projects daily; finish before optimizing.
Delayed GratificationChoose slow rewards that actually change life.Build research depth, English ability, presentation skill, fitness, and reading.
AgencyMove before certainty.Build, send, apply, test, and learn instead of simulating rejection.
Relentless ClosureOpen loops should become artifacts.Convert ideas into pages, slides, demos, papers, emails, or code.

Representative mottos

  • Success is continuity.
  • Excitement is direction; artifacts are proof.
  • Do not betray the person you could become.
