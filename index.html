Research

I am passionate about building models that don’t yet exist — models that reconstruct, animate, and generate dynamic 3D/4D content, especially for human and animals. Ultimately, my goal is to push the boundaries of 3D and 4D reconstruction and generation for dynamic objects. I’m driven by the desire to explore fun and meaningful research-creating tools and models that open up new creative and scientific possibilities.
